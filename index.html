Workshop Overview

Welcome to the Navident Advanced Surgical & Prosthetic Workshop—a comprehensive hands-on training for dental professionals who want to stay ahead of the curve in precision dentistry. Presented by Marks Biotech Inc & Claronav Dental - Canada., this workshop will showcase the latest advancements in dynamic navigation technology, enhancing surgical accuracy and patient outcomes in dental implantology and endodontics.

As technology transforms dentistry, clinicians who embrace these innovations will lead the future. Our expert-led workshop aims to equip you with cutting-edge tools, from AI and robotics to augmented reality and photogrammetry, empowering you to achieve predictable, precise results in complex dental procedures.

Is this a virtual or physical workshop?
This is a live physical workshop which you have to attend in person.
Is this workshop suitable for individuals without prior experience in digital dentistry?
No. This workshop is an advance level only for aspirants who want to transform their clinical practice completely digital. The participants need to have a minimum understanding of CBCT, Intraoral scanners, and guided surgery protocols.
Who is this workshop intended for?
This workshop is designed for dentists who intend to learn and explore the diverse fields of computer-guided implant surgery, guided endodontics, and complex surgery protocols with minimally invasive protocols to expand their practice, both surgical and prosthetics including lab procedures using Artificial Intelligence(AI), Augmented Reality(AR) and Image-Guided Navigation Technologies in everyday clinical practice.
Will I receive any certifications upon completion of the workshop?
Yes, participants will receive a certificate of participation from Claronav Dental Canada the Global Innovative Leaders In Robotics Technology.
